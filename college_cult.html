Student Registration/ Verification System

  • Student can add profile picture only once a year. Re-addressal sytems will be in place on need basis
  • As a first criteria, an email will be sent to the student's college email id and student needs to verify.
  • A live picture with ID will need to be uploaded for verification purpose.
  • Student can have a nickname.
  • One of the fundamental criteria on which the app is based and marketed will be on Eliminating Anonymous personnel who can use the app. This system will be verifying the student's info like College, Branch, Year etc., as strongly as a KYC we do for banks. Possible necessary practical actions need be designed and implemented.
  • Is Username auto-generated or User selects ?
  • Which name will be displayed on all live feeds or comments - username/nickname?
  • When someone updates their nickname/name it would affect all older posts as well. Is that acceptable ?
  • Everyone gets a QR code generated after successful regitration and it can be used for attending events, chatting etc.,

Live Feed of Student Posts/Events:

  • This is a home page for all students where they get a glimpse of what all is happening in and around all colleges. There will be a filter available for students to see :
    • Only Their College and their Branch
    • Only Their College
    • Only Their City or Town
    • Only Country
    • Everyone (Is this necessary ?)
  • Student can tag only ones who are in their friend list.
  • Comments can be added by anyone to all public posts
  • Likes and upvote/downvote can be provided to posts
  • Post can be made as private/ public
  • Define Private post and public post ?
    • Like private in the sense of College/Branch/friendCategory( like music playlist) ?
    • Like public in the sense, visible to entire population of audience ?
  • Posts can be Photos/ Text/ Videos/ Blogs/ External Links
    • (But how can we avoid students using this app as a dumping yard ?)
  • External links can be hateful, how do we address these ?
  • What is the ranking mechanism for posts ?
    • Is it by timestamp of updatedTimeStamp ?
    • What happens if one of your friend comments ? Is the post shown to the student on the top ? Does commenting update updatedTimeStamp for the post ?
  • Can a post be muted ? Likes, Comments, upvote/downvote disabled ?
  • Events will have Attend/Register button and organizer gets the related information.

Event Lifecycle System:

  • Event has a creator who is knwon as Organizer. Can there be multiple organizers to an event ?
  • Organizer creates a logo/ profile picture of the event/ title/ description/ Start - End Dates/ Timings
  • Upon submit of the creted Event, a workflow will be generated and gets submitted to Volunteer for approval.
  • Volunteer on checking with College guidelines publishes the event.
  • There should be a possiblity of back and forth discussions (like in git pull request).
  • Upon approval post gets published as per visibility options asked by Event Organizer
  • Event gets closed after the time out. Still students can comment on it. Or Is it ?
  • There are badges given to the Organizer upon successful event conducted.
    • How do you measure success ? Or is necessary to track ?
  • Do we need Escalation mechanism in case of Volunteer and Event Organizer differences ?
  • Organizers have the authority to publish winner/runner_ups/consolations after event is completed.

Volunteers

  • Volunteers need to be hired who are part of every College.
    • Every department needs one or 1 per college ?
  • Only when Event Organizer puts a request, Volunteer gets to see, discuss and approve.
  • They will have Open Requests/ Closed Requests/ Rejected Requests/ InProgress Requests
  • Volunteers can go to the event to collect/ scan QR codes. Or can he delegate this attendance check to other students ?
